Nepali Pork Momos

The Only Dumpling That Matters

I remember growing up and having alot of of parents' friends coming over for parties and i wouldn't be able to socialize with them so instead I made dumpling side by side with them to connect. It's one of my fondest memories.

Ingredients

  • Dumpling Flour Wraps
  • Ground Pork
  • Fresh Coriander
  • 1 large white onion
  • salt

Recipe

  1. Dump everything but the dumpling wrap into a large metal bowl.
  2. Wrap it up.
  3. Eat it.
