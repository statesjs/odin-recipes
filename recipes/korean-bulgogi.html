Korean Bulgogi



Beef au la Childhood

One of the all time favorite meals growing up in a korean restaurant. The sweetness of the beef mixed
with the refreshing taste on scallions makes for the perfect meal anytime of the day.

Ingredients

  • Beef
  • Green Onions
  • Soy Sauce
  • Sugar
  • Sesame Seeds
  • Sesame Oil
  • Pepper

Recipe

  1. Place the flank steak in a shallow bowl. In a separate bowl, combine the rest of the ingredients and whisk until blended. Pour the marinade over the steak â€” make sure the meat is completely covered. Cover the bowl and allow the beef to marinate in the fridge for about an hour.
  2. On a lightly oiled grill set to high heat, cook the beef until it's charred and completely cooked through. This should take about 1 or 2 minutes on each side.
  3. Finally season with fresh cut green onions for a more crisp taste and smell.
