Spicy Ahi Poke Bowl

Poke Bowls are Sushi Cereal in a Nutshell

Living in the pacific northwest has given me alot of oppurtunities to taste and try out the newest and the best cuisines across the world and this dish is one of my all time favorites.

Ingredients

  • 16 oz sashimi grade ahi tuna, cut into 1/2 inch cubes
  • 2 teaspoons of kikkoman soysauce
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 1 teaspoon of masago
  • cooked short grain rice

Recipe

  1. In a large bowl, add ⅓ Cup Kewpie Mayo, 4 Teaspoons Sriracha, 2 Teaspoons J apanese Soy Sauce, ½ Teaspoon Sesame Oil, 1 Tablespoon Masago and stir to combine. Add the cubed Ahi Tuna and toss to coat. Cover and refrigerate for 15 minutes or until you’re ready to serve.
  2. Add rice to a serving bowl, sprinkle on Furikake, top with a gen erous amount of the Spicy Ahi Poke, garnish with chopped green onions and Enjoy!
